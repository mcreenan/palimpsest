# Getting started with Demo Co

Create an **organization**, invite teammates, and pick a plan.

1. Sign up and create your org — you become the **owner**.
2. Invite members from **Settings → Members**. Roles are owner, admin, and member —
   see [Members & roles](members-and-roles).
3. Choose a plan on the **Billing** page (Free, Team, or Enterprise) —
   see [Billing & plans](billing).

Your org is the top-level container for everything: members, data, and billing all
belong to it. If you sign in with your company's identity provider, your admin may
have set up [single sign-on](sso-saml) for you instead of a password.
