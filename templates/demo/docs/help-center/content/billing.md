# Billing & plans

Demo Co offers three plans: **Free**, **Team**, and **Enterprise**. Each plan
includes a monthly quota of usage; usage above the quota is billed as overage at
the end of the cycle.

| Plan       | Monthly price | Included usage  |
|------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Free       | $0            | 1,000 units     |
| Team       | $49           | 25,000 units    |
| Enterprise | $499          | 500,000 units   |

## How invoices work

- Invoices are generated **per organization** at the end of each billing cycle.
- An invoice moves through `draft → open → paid`. A cancelled invoice becomes `void`.
- The amount is your plan's base price plus any overage above the included quota.
- Update your card on **Settings → Billing**. A failed charge retries for **7 days**
  before the invoice is marked overdue.

## Changing plans

Upgrade or downgrade any time on **Settings → Billing**. Upgrades take effect
immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of your next cycle so you keep
what you've paid for.

Contact support if an invoice looks wrong — we reconcile every invoice against
your metered usage. See also [Usage & limits](usage-and-limits).
