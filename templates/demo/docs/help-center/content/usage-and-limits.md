# Usage & limits

Your plan includes a monthly quota of **units**. A unit is one metered action —
see your dashboard for the live count. Usage resets at the start of each billing
cycle.

| Plan       | Included units / month |
|------------|------------------------|
| Free       | 1,000                  |
| Team       | 25,000                 |
| Enterprise | 500,000                |

## Tracking usage

**Dashboard → Usage** shows your usage for the current cycle and how it compares to
your included quota. The number updates continuously as you use the product.

## What happens when you go over

You're never cut off mid-cycle. Usage above your included quota is billed as
**overage** on your next invoice. If you regularly exceed your quota, upgrading is
usually cheaper than paying overage — see [Billing & plans](billing).

## Usage alerts

Owners and admins can turn on an alert at **Settings → Notifications** to get an
email when usage crosses a threshold (for example, 80% of your quota). See
[Email & webhook notifications](notifications).
