# Members & roles

Everyone in your organization has one of three roles. Roles control what a person
can do; they don't change what they can see of their own account.

| Role     | Can do                                                            |
|----------|------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Owner    | Everything, including billing and transferring ownership.        |
| Admin    | Invite/remove members, change member roles, manage settings.     |
| Member   | Use the product. Cannot manage billing or other members.         |

There is exactly **one owner** per organization.

## Inviting teammates

Owners and admins invite people from **Settings → Members → Invite**. Enter an
email and pick a role; the person gets an invite email and joins your org when they
accept. (Members don't see the invite button.)

## Changing a role

On **Settings → Members**, open the menu next to a person and choose a new role.

## Transferring ownership

The owner can hand off ownership on **Settings → Members**: promote another member
to **Owner**. You become an **admin** in the process — an org always has exactly
one owner. This is the right step before someone leaves the company.

## Removing a member

Admins and owners can remove a member from **Settings → Members**. Their access
ends immediately. Anything they created stays with the org.
